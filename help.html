Help

Welcome to the Gecomment-Help.


Create Comment

Create a new comment with your geodata. To submit a comment, fill out the formular. Choose a short, explained title. Copy your geodata-url, which can be a WMS,WMTS,KML,WFS or GML in the url-field. If you want to comment a specific point of interest, click into the map to get the coordinates. You can make your comment easier to find, if you tag it. Tags are words your comment is about. Also add a date to your submission. Without the date nobody will know for which timestamp or timespan your submission is.
Example: Hochwasser, Geistviertel, MÃ¼nster etc.

Sign-in

Zakbum is based on comments. For users comments are more expressive and easier to follow, if the comment is signed with a username. Register to Zakbum with a username, email-adress and a password.

Filter

In Zakbum you are able to filter the comments by several factors:
Type in a keyword, which describes your desired destination and you will receive autosuggestions from the database.
You can filter the comments by giving a specific range for the rating of a comment (Between 0 and 5).
Choose a time span, for example, if you want to find comments for August 2014 you should pick the limits 01.08.2014 and 31.08.2014.
Filter comments by choosing an area!
Bounding Box: Use a feature with four corners or give four corner-coordinates by tiping them in, to set a bounding box, in which you want to search.
Area: You can also set a point of interest and give it a certain radius (distance) around that point in which you want to search for comments.

Rating

Zakbum is a collaborative platform to comment on geodata and rate it. It is everybody's interest to get the best geodata possible. To see which comment or commented geodata set is good for you to work with, the rating helps you. The more users, espescially users with spatial cognitives about the area of a comment or experts, who rate data and/or answer on a comment, the better will these plattform get.
So feel free to rate geodata or comments about it in the most honest way, to help other users out.
